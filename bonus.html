Bonus — ECI 3-Stage Simulation

3-Stage Rocket to the ISS

A Soyuz-type 3-stage rocket launches from the equator and reaches the International Space Station at 408 km orbit. Full ECI simulation including gravity turn, staging, Hohmann transfer and circularisation burn.

Target: ISS · 408 km LEO ECI frame · Leapfrog + Euler 54 200 kg total · 500 kg payload Hohmann transfer
🛸
Mission Architecture
Soyuz-type, scaled for a 500 kg payload
Stage 1 — booster (LOX/Kero)
T = 800 kN · Isp = 290 s · 40 t prop
Stage 2 — upper stage (LH₂/LOX)
T = 200 kN · Isp = 348 s · 8 t prop
Stage 3 — manoeuvre / RCS
T = 2 kN · Isp = 300 s · 500 kg prop
Total initial mass
54 200 kg
Drag cutoff (Kármán line)
100 km — no drag above
Gravity turn profile
3 km → 80 km (0° → 90°)
Simplifications: equatorial launch (no inclination change), pre-programmed gravity turn (no GNC feedback), instantaneous staging, single Aero cross-section throughout. ISS rendezvous = velocity matching at 408 km — proximity ops not simulated.
🔬
Mission Phases
6 distinct phases from launch to docking
① Stage 1 burn — atmospheric ascent
800 kN thrust with gravity turn from 3 km to 80 km. Stage 1 jettisoned after ~57 s at ~40 km altitude.
② Stage 2 burn — orbital insertion
200 kN continues the gravity turn into vacuum. Burnout at ~150 km — Stage 2 jettisoned.
③ Coast — suborbital arc to apogee
Free ballistic coast (leapfrog integrator) to the first apogee. Stage 3 ignites here.
④ Circularisation + Hohmann burn 1
Stage 3 circularises the parking orbit, then applies a prograde Δv to raise apogee to 408 km (Hohmann perigee burn).
⑤ Hohmann transfer coast
Coast on the transfer ellipse until reaching 408 km altitude (apogee of transfer orbit).
⑥ Circularisation at ISS + Rendezvous
Second prograde Δv circularises at ISS altitude. One full orbital period completed → docking successful.

"""
bonus_analysis.py — Entry point for the 3-stage ISS mission.
Imports the physics engine from bonus_multistage.py and
the plotting functions from bonus_plot.py.
"""
from bonus_multistage import simulate_multistage, print_mission_summary
from bonus_plot import plot_mission, plot_orbit
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

# Run full ECI simulation
# dt_atm=0.1s in atmosphere, dt_space=5s in vacuum
data = simulate_multistage(dt_atm=0.1, dt_space=5.0)

# Print mission summary (events, orbital elements, Δv budget)
print_mission_summary(data)

# Figure 1: 4-panel timeline (altitude, speed, mass, phase portrait)
fig1 = plot_mission(data)

# Figure 2: 2-D ECI orbital view (x-y equatorial plane)
fig2 = plot_orbit(data)

plt.show()
